## ðŸš€ Quick Tips

If you want to solve levels without looking up the answer, try these "Audits":

1. **Text Audit**: Tap the words in the prompt; they might be interactive.
2. **Gesture Audit**: Try pinching, dragging with two fingers, or shaking your device.
3. **Permission Audit**: If the level implies sound or camera, check if you granted the app permissions.
